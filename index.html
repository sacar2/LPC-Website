London Prestige Car Wash is a full service car wash and detailing center located in London, Ontario.
Each car that goes through our carwash is cleaned with foam brushes, fresh water, and quality products, then hand dried with cotton towels.
Our hard working staff strive to deliver quality work and spotless vehicles, satisfaction guaranteed.

Come visit us and experience the best car wash in London!
Open 6 days a week; weather permitting and excluding major holidays.
