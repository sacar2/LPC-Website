London Prestige Car Wash is a full service carwash and detailing center located in London, Ontario. Our hard working staff strive to deliver quality work and spotless vehicles, satisfaction guaranteed.

Each car that goes through our car wash is cleaned with foam brushes, freshwater, and quality products, then hand dried with cotton soft-cloth towels.

Come visit us and experience the best car wash in London!

 Open 7 days a week; weather permitting and excluding major holidays.
We are NOT affiliated with the coin carwash located near our property.
