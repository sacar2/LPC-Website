London Prestige Car Wash is a full service car wash and detailing center located in London, Ontario. Our hard working staff strive to deliver quality work and spotless vehicles, satisfaction guaranteed.

Each car that goes through our carwash is cleaned with foam brushes, fresh water, and quality products, then hand dried with cotton towels.

Come visit us and experience the best car wash in London!

Open 7 days a week; weather permitting and excluding major holidays.

Services and Pricing

Car wash packages are a walk-in service that takes approximately 15-25 minutes to complete.
Detailing services (i.e. shampooing) require an appointment; time varies for detailing services.
